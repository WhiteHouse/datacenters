Data Center Optimization Initiative Community of Practice

Agencies can participate in a forum to collaborate on their data center optimization strategies and to discuss topics including OMB reporting best practices, data center infrastructure management, data center software and hardware procurement, and shared services marketplace participation.

The COP will hold meetings the first Wednesday of each month. GSA building in downtown Washington, D.C (the 1800 F Street location) from 10:00am until 12:00pm EST.

Join the Data Center's listserv to be updated on CoP opportunities by emailing listserv@listserv.gsa.gov with no subject and "subscribe datacenters" in the body.
